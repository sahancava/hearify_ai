About Hearify.AI

What is Hearify.AI?

Starting from the COVID-19 pandemic, people have been experiencing online speech platforms that bringing people together around some specified topics. These platforms are now one of the most popular places that the people are talking about what interest them. Hearify.AI allows people to get the real-time speech to text data from the platforms it integrates.

How does Hearify.AI work?

Hearify.AI does have nodes that are listening to the online streams listed below in real-time. Having the specified stream room has ended, Hearify.AI extracts the entire speech script and then goes to its AI module to summarize the script. In the meantime, it builds a big data that contains data such as;
'Which technology company name was mentioned the most in the last one month in the Twitter Spaces rooms in Japan?'
