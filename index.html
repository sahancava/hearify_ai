About Hearify.AI

What is Hearify.AI?

Starting from the COVID-19 pandemic, people have been experiencing online speech platforms that bringing people together around some specified topics. These platforms are now one of the most popular places that the people are talking about what interest them. Hearify.AI allows people to get the real-time speech to text data from the platforms it integrates.

Additionally, Hearify.AI offers data visualization and analytics tools to help users interpret and make use of the big data they collect from the various supported platforms. These tools could include charts, graphs, and other visual representations of the data, as well as analytics features such as trend analysis, sentiment analysis, and more.

How does Hearify.AI work?

Hearify.AI does have nodes that are listening to the online streams listed below in real-time. Having the specified stream room has ended, Hearify.AI extracts the entire speech script and then goes to its AI module to summarize the script. In the meantime, it builds a big data that contains very complex data such as;
  • Speech Summaries
  • Keywords
  • Topics
  • Company Names
  • Locations
  • Voice Records
  • Metadata
  • Entire Speech
  • User Behaviours
  • User Preferences

How does $Hearify.AI process?

Hearify.AI uses 15 starting nodes on AWS to listen to the most popular Twitter Spaces streams. Once the streams are finished, our main module extracts the entire script, and then our modules use ChatGPT's API to summarize the entire script. In addition to this, Hearify.AI stores various types of data, such as keywords, topics, company names, locations, voice records, metadata, entire speech, user behaviors, and user preferences on databases on AWS. This data is processed and stored securely to protect user privacy and is used to provide valuable insights for businesses and organizations.
